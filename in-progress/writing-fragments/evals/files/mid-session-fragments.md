# Untitled: the neighbor thing

The neighbor's dog barks the same three notes every morning at 6:15. I used to hate it. Now I set my alarm two minutes before so I can hear it land.

---

I keep coming back to the idea that noticing is a discipline, not a gift. People who "notice things" are just people who practiced noticing.

---

My grandmother used to say you can tell how someone feels about their life by how they describe their commute.

---

A leading word for this: **rehearsed noticing** — attention you've drilled into a habit until it looks like instinct.
