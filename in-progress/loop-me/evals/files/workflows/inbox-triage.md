# Inbox triage

**Trigger:** event — a new email lands in the "Support" Gmail label.

**Steps:**
1. Classify the email as bug report, billing question, or spam.
2. Spam is archived immediately.
3. Bug reports and billing questions get a drafted reply.

**Checkpoint:** before sending a reply.
